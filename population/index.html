Population of Kentucky per Sq. Mile by County (2000)


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Darion

This map was created using population estimate and county polygon data for the year 2000 provided by Kentucky Geological Survey (http://www.uky.edu/KGS/gis/bounds.html) and bluegrass county region data provided by U.S. Department of Commerce, U.S. Census Bureau, Geography Division, TIGER/Line Shapefile (https://www.census.gov/geo/maps-data/data/tiger-data.html). Map was produced with QGIS 2.18.16 software on Ubuntu 16.04. All of the datasets were referenced to the NAD83 EPSG:3089 CRS. (1:1,000,000)

The purpose of this thematic map is to illustrate the patterns of Kentucky population density by county and their general spatial relationship to the bluegrass region.
