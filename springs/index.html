Authored by: Darion

This map was created using spring data provided by Kentucky Geological Survey (ftp://data.gis.eppc.ky.gov/shapefiles/dow_groundwater_springs.zip) and Kentucky and bluegrass county data provided by U.S. Department of Commerce, U.S. Census Bureau, Geography Division, TIGER/Line Shapefile (https://www.census.gov/geo/maps-data/data/tiger-data.html). Map was produced with QGIS 2.18.16 software. All of the datasets were referenced to the NAD83 EPSG:3089 CRS. (1:1,000,000)

The purpose of this thematic map is to illustrate the areas of high groundwater spring density and their general spatial relationship to the bluegrass region and surrounding counties.
