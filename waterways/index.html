Waterways of Kentucky


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Darion

This map was created using data acquired from the National Atlas of the United States and QGIS 2.18.15 software on Ubuntu 16.04. All of the datasets were referenced to the NAD83 EPSG:3089 CRS. Only streams of Horton-Strahler Order 2-7 are displayed.

The purpose of this thematic map is to illustrate the patterns of Kentucky streams and waterbodies and their general spatial relationship to urban areas.
