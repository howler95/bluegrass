World map of Population Density and Internet Usage (2014)


Link to high resolution map

Authored by: Darion

This map was created using data acquired from the Natural Earth and The World Bank Group and QGIS 2.18.16 software on Ubuntu 16.04. All of the datasets were referenced to the World Mollweide CRS (EPSG:54009).

The purpose of this thematic map is to illustrate the patterns of world population and their relative internet usage.
